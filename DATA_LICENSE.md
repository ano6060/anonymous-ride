This applies to the released RIDE Silver, Gold Lite, and Gold Standard dataset
artifacts.

## Source Data

RIDE is derived from open railway operational data and open weather data:

- Infrabel source data are published through Infrabel Open Data under the CC0
  Universal open license.
- Open-Meteo API data are provided under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0
  International license (CC BY 4.0).

The RIDE dataset license is CC BY 4.0 to preserve the attribution requirements
of the Open-Meteo-derived weather variables while remaining compatible with the
CC0 status of the Infrabel source data.

Users of RIDE should attribute RIDE, Infrabel, and Open-Meteo, and should not
imply endorsement by any source-data provider.
